What this is

I started writing because I kept encountering theoretical biology papers that felt exciting but informal — intuitions dressed as arguments. Learning mathematics gave me a way to be more precise, but precision can make writing cold. The blog is an attempt to hold both: rigorous ideas, written for someone who cares about understanding rather than just following steps.

I also write poetry occasionally — in Hindi and in English. That writing lives elsewhere, more privately.
